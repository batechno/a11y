Turning TalkBack On/Off

  • Quick Toggle: Press both Volume Up and Volume Down buttons together for 3 seconds (if enabled).
  • Using Google Assistant: Say "Turn on TalkBack" or "Turn off TalkBack".
  • Settings: Settings > Accessibility > TalkBack.

Basic Navigation

  • Explore by Touch: Drag your finger around the screen to hear what is under your finger.
  • Swipe Right: Move to next item.
  • Swipe Left: Move to previous item.
  • Double-Tap Anywhere: Activate the currently focused item.

Gesture Highlights

  • Swipe Up or Down: Change navigation setting (e.g., Headings, Links, Controls).
  • Swipe Up then Right: Open TalkBack Menu.
  • Swipe Down then Left: Go back to previous screen.
  • Two-Finger Swipe Up/Down: Scroll up or down through a page or list.

Reading and Navigation

  • Read From Top: Swipe Down then Right, then select "Read from top".
  • Read From Next Item: Swipe Down then Right, then select "Read from next item".

Editing Text

  • Move Insertion Point: Swipe Left or Right in a text field.
  • Delete Character: Swipe Left then Down.

Other Useful Tips

  • TalkBack Tutorial: Settings > Accessibility > TalkBack > Settings > Launch tutorial.
  • TalkBack Braille Keyboard: Available under TalkBack Settings if needed.
