Dragon NaturallySpeaking Quick Start Guide

Updated for Dragon Professional Individual and Dragon Home 16 (2024). Focused on voice navigation and basic dictation.

Starting and Stopping Dragon

  • Start Dragon: Launch Dragon from Start Menu or Desktop.
  • Turn Microphone On/Off: Say "Wake up" or "Go to sleep".
  • Exit Dragon: Say "Close Dragon" or manually quit from system tray.

Basic Dictation Commands

  • Dictate Text: Speak naturally â€” Dragon will transcribe into active text field.
  • New Line: Say "New line".
  • New Paragraph: Say "New paragraph".
  • Correct Word: Say "Correct [word]".
  • Delete Word: Say "Scratch that" (undo last action).

Basic Navigation Commands

  • Open Application: Say "Open [Application Name]" (e.g., "Open Microsoft Word").
  • Switch Applications: Say "Switch to [Application Name]".
  • Click Button or Link: Say "Click [Button/Link Name]".
  • Scroll Page: Say "Scroll down" or "Scroll up".

Working with Web Pages

  • Activate a Link: Say "Click [link text]".
  • Move to Next/Previous Field: Say "Next field" or "Previous field" (on forms).

Editing and Formatting

  • Select Text: Say "Select [text]".
  • Bold/Italic/Underline: Say "Bold that", "Italicize that", "Underline that".

Other Useful Commands

  • Open Help: Say "What can I say?"
  • Launch DragonBar: Use DragonBar for microphone control and menus.
